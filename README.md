## 🇬🇧 English

**OnePot** is a free web app for recipes, meal planning and shopping lists.

Everything runs **100% locally on your device** — your data never leaves your browser.

### Benefits

* ✅ Free forever
* ✅ No account required
* ✅ No tracking
* ✅ Works offline
* ✅ Your data stays yours
